# About Knodeledge

**Knodeledge&#x20;**&#x69;s an **AI-powered** workspace that turns raw notes into an **interactive, self-linking knowledge graph**. Just dump your ideas in—our AI automatically extracts the concepts, maps their relationships, and builds your graph in real-time. It’s a living map of your thoughts that you can manually customize and chat with directly.



# Core Features

* **Bounded Workspaces**: Group your thoughts inside a dedicated, bounded scope. Define your project or research area, dump in your data, and let the engine organize everything within that context.
* **AI-Powered Graph Extraction**: The AI automatically breaks down your notes into individual, informational nodes complete with summaries and attachments. It then analyzes the existing graph to seamlessly create relationships between newly added concepts and old ones.
* **Interactive Manual Customization**: Keep complete control over your data. Easily create custom nodes manually, drag-and-drop to link concepts together, edit properties, and refine the graph to match your exact thinking.
* **Graph-Contextual AI Chat**: Chat directly with your workspace. Ask questions, and the AI uses the full context of your interconnected graph to provide synthesized, multi-hop answers, visually tracing the path it took across your nodes.
